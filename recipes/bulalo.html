Bulalo Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 kg beef shanks
  • 3 pcs corn
  • 1 lemon grass
  • 1 ginger
  • 1 inion
  • 4 cups of water
  • 2 tbps salt
  • ground pepper
  • 1 chinise Pechay
  • 4 pcs chopped potatoes

Steps

  1. Boil the meat with lemon grass and pepper until its tender
  2. When it tender put giner onion potatoes and corn let it boil again
  3. put salt and other seasoning and chinise pechay
  4. Ready to serve enjoy!
